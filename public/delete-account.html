Delete Your InstaGRE Account

InstaGRE, developed and published by InstaGRE

You can permanently delete your InstaGRE account and all data associated with it at any time, either from inside the app or by emailing us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open InstaGRE and sign in
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm by tapping Yes, Delete

This deletes your account immediately — no waiting period.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email (no app access needed)

If you've lost access to the app or your device, email aivisionlabs555@gmail.com from any address and include the mobile number registered on your InstaGRE account. We will verify the request and delete your account within 30 days.
